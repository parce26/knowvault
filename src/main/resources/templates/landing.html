Features
Everything your team needs
A complete platform to centralize, search and query your organization's knowledge — all in one place.
Document Management
Upload PDFs with title and category. Text is automatically extracted and indexed for AI retrieval the moment you upload.
AI-Powered Q&A
Ask questions in natural language. The RAG pipeline retrieves the most relevant chunks and Claude answers exclusively from your documents.
Query History
Every AI interaction is saved with the question, response, source documents, and response time. Search and delete entries anytime.
Process
How KnowVault works
Three steps to transform your documents into instant, intelligent answers.
1
Step one
Upload your documents
Upload PDF files from the administration panel. Each file is validated, stored securely, and queued for processing.
2
Step two
The system indexes the content
Text is extracted with Apache PDFBox and divided into chunks of ~400 words, stored in the database for retrieval.
3
Step three
Ask and get answers
Type a question in natural language. KnowVault finds the relevant chunks and Claude generates a precise answer from your content.
